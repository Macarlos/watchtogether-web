Legal & Privacy

Last updated: July 2026

What Reel is

Reel is a free tool that helps you find something to watch. You pick your streaming platforms and preferred mood, swipe through suggestions, and save the ones you like. There's no account, no sign-up, and nothing to install.

No accounts, no cookies

Reel does not require or offer account creation, and does not use cookies or tracking scripts of any kind. We keep one anonymous, aggregate counter of how many times the app has been opened and which platforms/genres get selected most — this is a simple running tally with no cookie, no identifier, and no way to connect it back to any individual visitor.

Your saved picks are stored locally in your own browser (not on our servers) so they're still there next time you visit. You can clear them anytime using the "Clear all picks" option in the app.

Shared picks links

If you use the "Share my picks" feature, Reel generates a link containing only the internal catalog IDs of the titles you liked — no personal information, no names, no identifying data of any kind. Anyone who opens that link can see which titles were included; nothing else about you is shared or stored.

Where the data comes from

Movie and TV information — titles, posters, descriptions, ratings, and streaming availability — is provided by Watchmode, a third-party data provider, via their API. Reel does not own, verify, or guarantee the accuracy of this data.

Streaming availability and pricing change frequently and can vary by region. Always confirm current availability and price directly on the streaming platform before making a purchase or rental decision.
